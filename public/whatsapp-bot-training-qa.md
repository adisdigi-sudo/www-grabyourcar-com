## 📋 SECTION 5: HSRP (High Security Registration Plate)

**Q: What is HSRP?**
A: HSRP stands for High Security Registration Plate. It's a tamper-proof, chromium-based hologram number plate mandated by the Government of India for all vehicles. It contains a unique laser code, hot-stamped chromium hologram, and a snap-lock mechanism that makes it non-reusable once removed.

**Q: Do you offer HSRP booking service?**
A: Yes! We offer hassle-free HSRP booking:
📋 Easy online booking
🏠 Doorstep fitment available
✅ Government-approved plates
⚡ Quick processing (7-15 days)
💰 Transparent pricing

Book now at: grabyourcar.lovable.app/hsrp

**Q: Is HSRP mandatory?**
A: Yes! HSRP is mandatory for ALL vehicles in India as per the Central Motor Vehicle Rules. New vehicles get HSRP from the dealer at registration. For older vehicles, you must get HSRP fitted to avoid penalties.

**Q: What is the penalty for not having HSRP?**
A: Not having HSRP can attract:
⚠️ Fine up to ₹5,000 for first offense
⚠️ Fine up to ₹10,000 for repeat offense
⚠️ Vehicle can be impounded

Avoid penalties — book your HSRP today!

**Q: How much does HSRP cost?**
A: HSRP pricing:
🚗 Two-wheeler: ₹300 - ₹500
🚗 Four-wheeler: ₹600 - ₹1,100
Prices may vary by state. Our team will share exact pricing for your vehicle.

**Q: How long does HSRP take?**
A: Typical timeline:
📋 Booking: Instant (online)
🏭 Manufacturing: 5-7 working days
🔧 Fitment: Scheduled appointment
Total: 7-15 working days

We also offer priority processing for urgent requirements!

**Q: What documents are needed for HSRP?**
A: Documents required:
📄 Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC)
📄 Valid ID Proof (Aadhar/DL)
📄 Vehicle chassis number
📄 Engine number
That's it! Our team will assist with everything.
